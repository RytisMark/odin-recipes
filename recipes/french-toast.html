French toast

Description

French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and typically milk, then pan fried. This French toast recipe is different because it uses flour. This is a recipe for 12 servings (slices).

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices of bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
